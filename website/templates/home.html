About e-Mark
Welcome to e-Mark, our prototype for digital watermarking on academic e-certificates. Our goal is to provide a simple, effective, and affordable solution for verifying the authenticity of academic credentials. Using our easy-to-use interface, you can quickly and easily embed a watermark onto a group of images, making it virtually impossible to alter or forge academic certificates. By overlapping words on images, we create a unique and tamper-proof identifier for each certificate, ensuring its authenticity. With e-Mark, employers and educational institutions can quickly and easily verify the authenticity of a certificate, without the need for complex verification procedures. Explore our prototype to see how we're making academic credentials more secure and reliable than ever before.     Watermark icons created by Freepik - Flaticon



Future Expansion
In order to enhance the usability of the system, additional features will be integrated in the near future. However, due to time limitations, certain complex functionalities, such as direct manipulation of images by adding custom shapes and text, along with extensive customization options, will not be included at this stage. While some features have already been developed and can be tested, please note that the system is still in development,and certain features may be incomplete or lacking in functionality.
