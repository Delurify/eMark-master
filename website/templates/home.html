About e-Mark
Welcome to e-Mark, our prototype for digital watermarking on academic e-certificates. Our goal is to provide a simple, effective, and affordable solution for verifying the authenticity of academic credentials. Using our easy-to-use interface, you can quickly and easily embed a watermark onto a group of images, making it virtually impossible to alter or forge academic certificates. By overlapping words on images, we create a unique and tamper-proof identifier for each certificate, ensuring its authenticity. With e-Mark, employers and educational institutions can quickly and easily verify the authenticity of a certificate, without the need for complex verification procedures.
